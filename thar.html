The Essence Of Great Thar Desert

Unveiling the Enigma

Thar desert or the Great Indian desert is one of the most important tourist destinations of Rajasthan. One can see the true Rajasthani culture in the desert. The folk song and dance of Rajasthan can be experienced in the desert. Jaisalmer also known as the “The Golden City” is one of the major attractions in the desert. It is located in the heart of the desert. It is known as the golden city because of the golden color sand dunes present in the city.

Desert National Park is located between Jaisalmer and Barmer. The Desert National Park consists of fossils of animals and plants of 180 million years old. It is home to some of the most exotic animals and birds like the Great Indian Bustard, desert fox, Bengal fox, Chinkara, Falcons, Short toed eagles, migratory birds. With a fragile ecosystem and rough sand dunes, rocks and compact salt lakes bottom, the Desert National Park is a must visit place in the thar desert.
